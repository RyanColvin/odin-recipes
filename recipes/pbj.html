Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich

Description

A yummy sandwich with peanut butter and Jelly

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Bread
  • Jelly of your choice(Grape is my favorite)
  • Knife and fork or spoon

Steps

  1. Get two pieces of bread side by side on the counter
  2. Use knife to get the peanut butter and spread it on one of the pieces of bread
  3. Use fork or spoon to get jelly and spread it on the other piece of bread
  4. Put the two pieces of bread together
  5. EAT IT!
