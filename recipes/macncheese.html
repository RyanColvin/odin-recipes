Mac n Cheese


Description

Mac n cheese is basically noodles covered in ooie gooie cheese, not sure if I spelt ooie right...

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • cheese
  • butter
  • milk

Steps

  1. Add noodles to the pot
  2. Add enough water so there is about an inch above the noodles
  3. Turn the heat on high until bowling, then lower a bit
  4. After 20 minutes test a noodle to make sure it's done
  5. Get a strainer and strain the noodles in the sink
  6. Add the noodles back to the pot
  7. Add butter to the noodles and stir until it's all melted
  8. Add the powdered cheese package and stir until mixed well
  9. Add a little milk at a time until it's just right
  10. EAT IT!
