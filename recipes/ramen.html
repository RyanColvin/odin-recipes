Ramen

Description

Not much to describe here, just buy a packet of ramen noodles at Walmart

Ingredients

  • Packet of ramen noodles

Steps

  1. Empty the package of noodles in a pot
  2. Don't empty the sauce packet yet!
  3. Add enough water, about an inch above the noodles
  4. Turn the heat on high until boils, then turn down a bit
  5. After 10 minutes test the noodles
  6. Empty as much water as you want in the sink - some like it with tons of juice, others only a little
  7. Add the sauce packet and stire
  8. EAT IT!
